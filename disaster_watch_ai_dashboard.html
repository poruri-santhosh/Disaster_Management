Project Overview

This dashboard presents an early warning system for flood risks, integrating environmental data with satellite imagery analysis. Our AI-driven approach aims to empower communities and emergency responders with timely, data-driven insights to mitigate the impact of natural disasters.

By identifying flood-prone areas and assessing current conditions, we strive to enable proactive measures, save lives, and protect property.
